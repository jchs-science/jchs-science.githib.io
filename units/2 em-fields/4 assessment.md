# The Assessment
## Assignment
For this project, you will be demonstrating your understanding of the interaction of electricity and magnetism by building a speaker or a fan. You may build a device or a model of a device or you may create a presentation explaining the working of the device.  In any case, you must be able to explain how the device works to the class.
